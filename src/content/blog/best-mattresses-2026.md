## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which mattress is best for back pain?
**Purple, Saatva Classic, or Avocado Green.** Medium-firm hybrids or latex tend to work best for back pain sufferers.

### What's the best mattress for side sleepers?
**Tempur-Pedic TEMPUR-Cloud or Helix Midnight.** Side sleepers need softer materials that cushion shoulders and hips.

### Which mattress sleeps the coolest?
**Brooklyn Bedding Aurora or Purple.** Hybrids with advanced cooling technology outperform memory foam.

### What's the best value mattress?
**Nectar Memory Foam.** $499 with lifetime warranty and 365-night trial is unbeatable value.

### How long should a mattress last?
**7-15 years depending on type.** Latex lasts longest (15-20 years), memory foam 7-10 years, budget options 3-5 years.

### Can I trust online reviews?
**Look for verified purchases and recent reviews.** Our testing is independent and we disclose affiliate relationships.
